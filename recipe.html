Recipe of the Week

Lemon Garlic Pasta

Time: 30 minutes Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 8oz fettucine pasta
  • 2 lemons
  • 8-10 cloves of garlic
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Oregano
  • Dried parsley

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Set aside, reserving some pasta water for laterâ‰¥
  2. 2. While your pasta is boiling, peel and mince your garlic. Dice your spring onions, and juice your lemons.
  3. 3. Add butter to a pan on medium heat. Once sizzling, add garlic and spring onions, stirring often.
  4. 4. Add lemon juice, along with pasta water for desired consistency and wait for sauce to come to a boil.
  5. 5. Now toss cooked pasta into sauce and coat well.
  6. 6. Garnish with salt, red pepper flakes, oregano and parsley to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
